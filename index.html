Our Donate Plan

Depending on the situation, our group group may need donations of food, water, place for stay, books or other supplies. So, we divided our plan into three parts.

Food for a family

Our volunteers will supply food related item home to home.

Shelter for a family

We will help homeless to go safe place or if needed we will help them repair their houses.

Education for a child

We will help by providing books and educational item to the effected students and who have exams nearby.
